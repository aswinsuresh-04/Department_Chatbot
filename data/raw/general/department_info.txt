The Department of Computer Science focuses on advanced education and research in computer science and information technology.

2. History of the Department

The Department of Computer Science was established in 1984. The first academic program started by the department was the M.Tech in Computer and Information Science. The department has since expanded into several research and postgraduate education areas.

3. Mission

The mission of the department includes:
- Promoting knowledge development in computer science
- Supporting research and innovation
- Providing modern computing infrastructure
- Producing skilled professionals for industry and academia

4. Academic Programs

M.Tech Programs:
- M.Tech in Computer and Information Science
- M.Tech in Software Engineering

Research Program:
- Ph.D in Computer Science

5. Research Areas
